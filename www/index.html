OpenCy is a new approach to computing focusing on integrating the information revolution with the broad spectrum of human experience in a holistic manner. OpenCy rejects the hyper-rationalistic, narrow approach to problem solving which dominates the tech industry today. The OpenCy way is to create tools which facilitates intuitive and analytical solutions to computational problems. The OpenCy inspired by the tradition of Douglas Eberhart, Paul Graham and others.
